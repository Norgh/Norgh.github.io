1) Controls

  • Go left => "Q"
  • Go right => "D"
  • Jump => "Z"
  • Replay, Pause => "Spacebar"
  • RageQuit => "Alt+F4"

2) How to play ?

You are a runner who aims to run as far as possible while dodging obstacles in his path. Obstacles are hedges that can be dodged by moving left or right or jumping.

To help you, bonuses (water, carrots, ...) will appear on your way. But beware, it can also have penalties that will bring you additional difficulties.

There you have it, you are now able to play the Skyline-Runner game. Good luck...
